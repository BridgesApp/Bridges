F.A.Q

  • Where can I download Bridges?
    Bridges is available in the App Store as well as Google Play for download
  • How can I update my contact information manually?
    Go to 'My Profile' and select 'Settings'. From there, you can change your contact info in 'Update my Info Manually'
  • What happens if my account gets hacked?
    By clicking 'Reset my Account' you can delete your account through the email or phone number you provided while signing up
  • How to I add contacts on Bridges?
    Go to 'Settings' and press 'Connect my Contacts' to connect your existing contacts on your device
  • How to I add contacts on Bridges?
    Go to 'Settings' and press 'Connect my Contacts' to connect your existing contacts on your device
  • What do I do if I forgot my Username/Password?
    If you go to the 'Login' section, and click 'Forgot Username/Password?' an email will be sent from where you can recover your account
  • How do I change my privacy settings?
    If you naviguate to 'Settings' and click on 'Privacy' you will be able to update any privacy settings
  • How do I activate my account?
    After setting up your account, an activation email will be sent to the email you signed up with
  • How do I update personal information (name, pronouns)?
    If you click 'Edit my Bio', any personal information other users may see can be updated
  • How do I access the Privacy Policy?
    If you naviguate to 'Terms of Use' you can access the Privacy Policy
