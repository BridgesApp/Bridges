Everything is Supported

Any and all contacts can be stored and shared! Including social media handles, phone numbers or even email addresses for more formal affairs.

NFC Technology

Using Near Field Communication technology, all it takes is a tap between 2 phones to instantly share contacts making it perfect for parties or networking events.

QR Compatability

With the support of QR codes, you can even have your contact on a printed piece of paper. Game changing for advertising or business cards.

Personalisation

Make your profile yours. Modify which information will be shared by default, write a biography, include a preffered method of contact and even share your nicknames or pronouns.

Automatic Updates

Bridges will automatically let your friends know when any of your contacts have changed. No more need to go through the trouble of telling everyone when you change your phone number.
